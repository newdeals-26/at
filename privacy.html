Privacy Policy

Last updated: 22 Aug 2025

Overview

BattleRush is a casual web gaming site. We do not require account signup and we minimize data collection.

Information We Handle

  • Local data: high scores and simple preferences stored in your browser’s localStorage. This never leaves your device unless you export or share it.
  • Contact messages: if you choose to contact us, we receive only the details you submit (e.g., name, email, message).

Cookies

We do not set cookies by default. If you integrate third-party tools later (e.g., analytics or ads), those services may set cookies under their own policies.

Your Choices

  • Clear site data: remove local scores and preferences using the button below.
  • Private browsing: use incognito/private windows to avoid storing local data.

Contact

Questions? Use the contact form on the Home page.
